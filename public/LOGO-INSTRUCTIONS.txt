HOLOCRYPT LOGO SETUP
====================

Save the HoloCrypt logo image (the second attached image with the shield and lock) as:
holocrypt-logo.png

Place it in this folder (public/).

The logo will automatically appear in the top-left corner of the landing page.

Recommended logo specifications:
- Format: PNG with transparent background
- Size: 200-400px width (will auto-scale to 60px height)
- Aspect ratio: Maintain original proportions
